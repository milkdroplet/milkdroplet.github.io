Maze Phase

Seeking the thrill of solving mazes in an arcade-style fast reaction game? Come phase through waves of mazes in this difficult and addicting experience! With simple and vibrant graphics, control your player with your finger and survive all the stages! Compete with your friends to see who is the best maze phaser! Can you be the perfect phaser and beat the game in one try? Maybe... Maybe not... Give it a shot!
